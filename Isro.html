About ISRO

ISRO is the national space agency of India for the purpose of all space based applications like reconnaissance & communications and doing research. It undertakes design and development of space rockets, satellites, explores upper atmosphere and deep space exploration missions

Eligibility criteria
ISRO 2021 Scientist/Engineer 'SC' Post Eligibility Criteria
(1) Intermediate Education Qualification: Intermediate/ Class 12th passed from the PCM Group along with a minimum of 65% marks.
(2) Graduate Education Qualification: E/B.Tech degree with at least 65% marks in aggregate or CGPA 6.84/10 (average of all semesters for which results are available).
(3) Final Year: Candidates who are in the final year of the qualifying degree can also apply provided they can produce the same by the time the final selections are made.
(4)The qualification prescribed and the benchmark is only the MINIMUM requirement and fulfilling the same does not automatically make candidates eligible for Written Test.
(5)Age Limit: 35 years as on March 5, 2021. Ex-Servicemen and Persons with Disabilities are eligible for age relaxation as per Govt. of India orders.
