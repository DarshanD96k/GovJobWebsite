About BARC

BARC is a multi-disciplinary research centre with extensive infrastructure for advanced research and development covering the entire spectrum of nuclear science, engineering and related areas. BARC's core mandate is to sustain peaceful applications of nuclear energy, primarily for power generation.

BARC Selection Process

* Based on the GATE score: Except for the "Nuclear Engineering," all other courses have the option of shortlisting candidates through their GATE score. Candidates with a good GATE score can apply and get shortlisted to the next interview round.

* Based on Online Examination: Candidates who have not appeared for the GATE exam or those who don't have a good GATE score need not worry. They can apply for the BARC 2021 exam and give their best. Candidates will be shortlisted to the next round (interview) based on their performance in the online examination.

Selection Interviews will be conducted in Mumbai for all disciplines except Geology and Geophysics. For Geophysics and Geology, the interviews will be conducted in Hyderabad. The shortlisted candidates who live outstation will be reimbursed with a two-way normal Tier-III AC train fare from the place they have registered their permanent address.

Candidates will be sent a notification about the selection process through emails and messages. The final selection will be subjected to medical fitness and the performance of the candidate in the personal interview. The candidates will be notified about their selection through SMS or Email.

BARC Exam 2021 Eligibility Criteria

1) B.E. / B.Tech. / B.Sc. (Engineering) / 5-year Integrated M.Tech. with a minimum of 60% aggregate marks.

2) Applicants opting to be considered based on a GATE Score must have a valid GATE-2020 or GATE-2021

Score in the same engineering discipline as the qualifying degree discipline.

3) Qualifying degree in branches like Aerospace, Automobile, Industrial Production, Reliability, Ceramics, Architecture, Geology, Mining, Bio-Medical Electronics/ Instruments, Communication, Information Technology, Master of Computer Applications, Dyes & Dye Intermediates, Electrochemical, Energy Systems, Oils & Fats, Paints & Varnishes, Petrochemicals, Plastics, Paper, Sugar Technology, Textiles, Mechatronics, etc. is not eligible.

How to fill BARC Application Form
A step-by-step process has been given below to aid you in apply BARC 2021 Exam:
  • Step 1: First of all, the candidates have to go to the official website of BARC 2021 Exam.
  • Step 2: Next, candidates need to click on "Apply Online" link and fill the appropriate information like Name, Mother's name, date of birth email ID etc.
  • Step 3: After the successful registration, candidates will receive an OTP on their registered mobile number or they will receive an activation link on registered email ID. They can use any of the two to activate their account.
  • Step 4: Post activation, the candidate can start to fill the application form. He/she also has to upload images of passport-sized photograph and signature. Size of the images should not exceed 50 KB for photograph file and 20 KB for the signature file.
  • Step 5: Next, a candidate will have to pay the application fee for the BARC exam which is INR 500/- (excluding taxes). Exam fees can only be paid through online mode. Candidates can use Credit Card/Debit Card/Internet Banking to pay the fee.
  • Step 6: At last, the candidate will be issued with a unique registration number. This number will be considered for all kinds of future correspondence.
