About Gate

GATE 2022 is a national-level test conducted for admission to postgraduate engineering (ME/ M.Tech) or research courses at top colleges in India such as IITs, NITs, IIITs, etc.

Eligibility criteria

GATE Eligibility Criteria 2022 will be released by IIT Kharagpur along with the official notification. The eligibility criteria was relaxed last year by IIT Bombay, allowing candidates currently in the third or higher year of their degree program to fill GATE Application Form along with candidates who have already completed any government-approved degree program in Engineering / Technology / Architecture / Science / Commerce / Arts.
