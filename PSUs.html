About PSUs

A government-owned enterprise in India is called a Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) or a Public Sector Enterprise. These companies are wholly or partly owned by the Government of India or one of the many state or territorial governments or both together in parts.

Eligibility criteria

Eligibility criteria for each PSU is different. It include factors like engineering field, percentage in BE/B.tech and upper age where percentage and age are relaxed for SC/ST and PH candidates. Apart from these factors since 2012 the number of PSUâ€™s recruiting engineers through GATE exam has increased so appearing for GATE exam has also become one of the eligibility criteria to get job in PSU for engineer.

Note that:Another important point for PSU recruitment through GATE is that these companies will accept the score of the present year only irrespective of the fact that GATE scores are valid for three years. GATE scores are not valid for three years for PSU recruitment.

How to Apply for PSU Recruitment through GATE

Soon after the commencement of GATE online registrations, application for PSU recruitment through GATE will be make available by the respective companies. Every PSU may have a separate application fee that candidates will be required to pay after submitting the filled-in application forms. The forms have to be filled with the correct information and candidates must upload a scanned image of their photograph and signature. Candidates will also require to provide their GATE registration number which will be present on the GATE application form (submitted finally) or on GATE admit card. Aspirants can check the tentative schedule of PSU Recruitment through GATE for below. The application date for PSU recruitment given in the table is tentative and will be updated just after the release of the schedule. Candidates can also check the number of posts likely to be available for PSU recruitment through GATE.
